Push to Apollo — Outlook Add-in

Push your Outlook reply into an Apollo sequence so follow-ups continue automatically.
Manifest URL for sideloading:
https://nicka2717.github.io/stormsearch-apollo-outlook-addin/manifest.xml

What it does

When you reply to an email in Outlook, this add-in adds a Push to Apollo button. Click it to send your typed reply into an Apollo sequence's manual email step, automatically — no copy/paste needed. Apollo handles all the follow-up steps from there.

Setup

  1. Sideload this manifest in your Outlook (admin → integrated apps → custom add-in)
  2. Open any email reply window in Outlook
  3. Click Push to Apollo
  4. Paste your Apollo API key in Settings (one-time, stored locally)
  5. Pick contact + sender mailbox + sequence → Push
